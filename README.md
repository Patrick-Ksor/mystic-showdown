# Mystic Showdown

A battle arena game featuring mythical creatures with elemental powers. Train your monsters, master unique moves, and conquer the gauntlet to prove your prowess as a trainer.

## Features

- **Battle Arena**: Engage in tactical turn-based combat
- **Monster Selection**: Choose from 24 unique monsters across 11 different element types
- **Move Tutor System**: Learn and manage strategic moves for your monsters
- **Gauntlet Mode**: Face progressively challenging opponents
- **Progression System**: Train and level up your monsters
- **Audio**: Dynamic sound effects and interactive mute controls
